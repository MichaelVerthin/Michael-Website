About

אודות

Hello, my name is Michael.

After 4 years of serving in the Israeli Navy, I searched for a workplace where I can hone my skills, challenge myself and learn new tricks. I found my place in QA, Quality Assurance, I studied the process of Software Testing and got invested in all of its aspects.

The most important quality assurance skills for me are Planning and delivering Results, I believe that I am ultimately responsible for delivering and maintaining the QA process consistently throughout everything I do, In my opinion, I have those skills and qualities and can be relied upon to deliver fantastic results

Except for Quality Assurance, I am a big programming enthusiast that loves to discover new programming languages and learn new algorithms to solve problems. Another thing that I absolutely adore is coffee, there is nothing better than a good cup of coffee in the morning.

Project Warehouse

פרוייקט מחסן

In this project, I was grouped into a team of 5,
Then we were tasked a made-up warehouse by our teachers, and had to create an STP and find bugs or issues and report them using a program named TestRail, it had a free trail so that was perfect for us to use for this one,

We were also given the product details of that warehouse, it was supposed to replace an old system that included using Microsoft Excel and handwritten information.

The new system we got in the product details contained all of the information including a detailed explanation about the users of the system, a technical overview of the majority of screens in the software, the system also has BATCH processes and Query option that connect to a database.

Project Web

פרוייקט אתר

In that project, my teachers tasked us to find a website that we need to find bugs or issues in and report them using a project management program named Jira, then they divided us into groups of 2, so after I found my partner we started to look for a buggy website.
we stumbled upon a school website that offered a promotional and school media website that didn't exactly offer the best experience, it also had some serious security issues, since everyone can access the website and browse, download and use the photos of the children that learn in that school, without any login.
After we finished checking the website in two circles, one using a pre-made checklist and the second by exploratory testing, we reported all the issued and bugs into Jira, made an STR document, and then presented it in a presentation
